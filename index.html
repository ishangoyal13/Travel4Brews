OOTY
Nestled amidst Nilgiri hills, Ooty, is a hill station in Tamil Nadu which serves as a top-rated tourist destination.Dotted with tea gardens, serene waterfalls, winding country lanes. Ooty allures its visitors with the panoramic views of the Nilgiri mountains.
GOA
Lying on the western coast, Goa is India's smallest state and unlike any other, it is best known for its white-sand beaches, stellar nightlife, eclectic seafood, world-heritage listed architecture. Best known for its "Beaches, Sunsets and Crazy Nights".
DARJEELING
Darjeeling is the most beautiful hill stations in Eastern part of India. Jaw-dropping locales, mesmerising sunrises, untouched beauty of the hills and the welcoming smiles of the local people all add up to make it worth while.
